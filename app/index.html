for Merchants

Save-O offers service stations a low cost alternative to pay-at-pump.

Customers gain payment pre-approval before commencing their fill and pay for their fuel purchase using their mobile phone:

  • Low-cost installation process.
  • Minimal hardware and infrastructure requirements.
  • No additional resources required – and helps reduce staff costs.
  • Allow out-of-hours purchases.
  • Keep track of purchases using a simple web dashboard.

Save-O allows you to search for petrol stations in your area and view real-time pricing.

You can select a station, reserve a pump and pay for fuel all from the convenience of your mobile phone without having to go in-store:

  • Save money by finding the cheapest fuel nearby when you’re out and about.
  • Save time by finalising your purchase at the pump without going in-store.
  • Keep track of your fuel purchases and view your transaction history in one place.
